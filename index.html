Superform is your go-to platform for building low-code microapps for whenever you want some input and some level of interactivity with your public, friends or teammates.

Superform has batteries included and it's easy to create interactive forms or even complex apps with just a little bit of HTML and data your visitors leave.

You write the UI for your microapp using our pluggable components, plus any HTML nodes you need using the super easy pug syntax.

People submit data through custom forms in your UI.

You use the data submitted, optionally preprocessing it first, in rendering the new UI, thus easily enabling all sorts of interactivity.

Get started by cloning our example microapps
